About Me

Welcome to TaskManagerApp

Our web application is designed to help you efficiently manage tasks and stay on top of your responsibilities. Whether you need to create new tasks, assign them to users, or track progress, our platform provides the tools you need for organized and productive task management.

With a user-friendly interface, you can:
- Add and update task details including title, description, due date, and category.
- Track task status from In Progress to Completed.
- Automatically receive reminders for upcoming deadlines.
Start managing your tasks more efficiently today!
